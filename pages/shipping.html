Shipping & Delivery

How delivery works

Lead time

Every order is hand-made fresh, so we need 3 to 4 days from when you place the order until delivery. You'll pick a delivery date at checkout from the available slots.

Delivery areas

  • Greater Cairo (Heliopolis, New Cairo, 5th Settlement, Madinaty, Maadi, Zamalek, Mohandessin, Dokki, Sheikh Zayed, 6th of October, Nasr City) — 30 EGP
  • Outside Cairo / Giza — message us on WhatsApp for a courier quote, especially for larger orders

Delivery slots

We deliver within these time windows. You choose your slot at checkout based on what's available.

  • Morning: 10:00 – 13:00
  • Afternoon: 14:00 – 17:00
  • Evening: 18:00 – 21:00

Slots fill up — book early for weekend dates.

What happens after you place the order

  1. Confirmation — you receive an email + (if you're on Telegram) a Telegram message.
  2. Deposit — you send the 50% deposit via InstaPay or Vodafone Cash. We confirm within 30 minutes.
  3. Preparation — our kitchen starts baking on the morning of your delivery date.
  4. Out for delivery — your rider gets the box. You receive a status update.
  5. Delivered — pay any remaining balance to the rider in cash (or by Vodafone Cash to the rider's number).

Packaging

Every order ships in our signature gift-style box. Insulated where needed for chocolate-heavy orders to survive Cairo summer heat.

Late deliveries

If your rider is more than 30 minutes late on the scheduled slot, we'll WhatsApp you proactively. If we miss your slot entirely without notice and you're unhappy, see our Returns & Refunds policy.
