Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 May 2026

What we collect

When you create an account or place an order on ChocoDoDo we collect: your name, email address, Egyptian mobile number, delivery addresses, order history, and any notes you leave for the kitchen.

How we use it

  • To deliver your order — the kitchen needs your address and phone to reach you; the rider may call to coordinate hand-off.
  • To send transactional emails — order confirmation, password resets, account verification.
  • To improve the service — aggregate, anonymous analytics on which products customers prefer.

We do not sell your data to third parties and we don't send marketing emails unless you explicitly subscribe to our newsletter.

Cookies

We use a single session cookie (cd_customer or cd_staff) to keep you signed in, plus your browser's localStorage to remember your cart between visits. No third-party trackers, no ad cookies, no Facebook pixel.

Payments

ChocoDoDo uses manual-confirmation payments (InstaPay, Vodafone Cash, Cash on Delivery). Your bank details never touch our servers — you transfer money in your own banking app and we confirm receipt manually.

Your rights

Under Egypt's Personal Data Protection Law (Law No. 151 of 2020), you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To do any of these, message us via WhatsApp or email and we'll respond within 7 business days.

Data retention

We keep order history indefinitely for accounting purposes. You can delete your account at any time from your account page; orders remain in our system but are no longer linked to a deleted account.

Contact

For privacy questions: WhatsApp our number or email Fromchocododo@gmail.com.
