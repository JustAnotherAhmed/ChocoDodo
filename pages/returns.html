Returns & Refunds

Returns & Refunds Policy

Last updated: 11 May 2026

We hand-make every order from scratch with no shelf stock. That makes returns work a little differently from a typical online shop.

Problem with your order on arrival?

If your order arrives damaged, wrong, or genuinely unsatisfactory in quality (e.g., burnt, broken, missing items), tell us within 2 hours of delivery via WhatsApp with a photo. We'll either:

  • Replace the affected items on our next delivery run, or
  • Refund the affected items by the same payment method (InstaPay/Vodafone Cash transfer back to you, or deduction from your next order).

Cancellations before delivery

When you cancelRefund
Same day you placed the order (before we start baking)100% of deposit refunded
1–2 days before delivery (mid-prep)50% of deposit refunded
Day of delivery (already baked / out for delivery)No refund

Custom orders

Custom designs, personalised messages, or bespoke flavors are non-refundable once we've started preparation, because we can't resell them.

What's not refundable

  • "Changed my mind" after the kitchen has started baking.
  • Personal taste preferences (e.g., "less sweet than I expected"). We can swap flavors on your next order — just tell us.
  • Failed delivery because you weren't reachable on the phone number provided.

How to request a refund or replacement

  1. WhatsApp our customer service number within 2 hours of delivery (or as soon as you cancel).
  2. Include your order ID and a photo if the issue is visible.
  3. We'll reply within 2 business hours and resolve within 3 business days.
