Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

How long does delivery take?

Every order is hand-made from scratch, so we need 3 to 4 days from when you place the order. You pick the exact delivery day at checkout.

Do you deliver outside Cairo?

Currently we deliver within Greater Cairo and Giza. For other governorates, WhatsApp us — we can sometimes arrange courier delivery for larger orders.

How much is delivery?

30 EGP flat fee within our standard delivery zones. Outlying areas may cost more — you'll see the exact total before you pay.

What payment methods do you accept?

InstaPay (any Egyptian bank), Vodafone Cash, or Cash on Delivery. A 50% deposit is required to confirm your order; the rest is paid on delivery.

Can I order for a specific time / occasion?

Yes! At checkout you pick a delivery slot. For weddings, engagements, or large events, WhatsApp us first so we can coordinate.

Are your products nut-free / gluten-free?

We have nut-free and lower-sugar options on the menu, but the kitchen handles all of them, so we can't guarantee a 100% allergen-free environment. If you have a serious allergy, please WhatsApp us before ordering.

How do I track my order?

You'll get a tracking link by email and Telegram (if you have a customer Telegram). You can also check the tracking page with your order ID anytime.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Yes — see the Returns & Refunds policy for the exact rules. The earlier you tell us, the more we can refund.

Do you do custom designs / printed photos / corporate gifts?

Yes — message us on WhatsApp with what you have in mind. Custom orders typically need 5–7 days lead time.

I didn't get an email after signing up. What now?

Check your spam folder first — Gmail often files brand-new senders there. If it's not there either, request a new verification email from your verification page.

Question not answered here? WhatsApp us — we typically reply within minutes during business hours.
